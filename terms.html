What Anchor is

Anchor is a personal mental health and wellbeing tool. It provides:

  • Daily mood and stress check-ins
  • A private journal
  • A personal safety plan template
  • Breathing and grounding exercises
  • Activity tracking and self-care reminders
  • Crisis resources and helpline signposting
  • Links to external tools and NHS services
